Sustainable Fishing

Protecting oceans • Supporting communities

Short, clear info to help consumers choose seafood that’s good for the environment.

What is sustainable fishing?

Sustainable fishing means catching fish at a rate where populations stay healthy, using methods that minimize harm to habitats and other species, and supporting fishers who follow responsible practices.

Certified sustainable brands

  • Marine Stewardship Council (MSC)
  • Aquaculture Stewardship Council (ASC)
  • GlobalG.A.P. (selected seafood)
  • Local cooperatives with sustainability labels

Guidelines for consumers

  • Look for trusted certifications (e.g., MSC, ASC).
  • Prefer seasonal and locally caught seafood.
  • Avoid threatened species — check regional seafood guides.
  • Ask vendors about gear and location (line-caught, small-scale).
  • Reduce waste: store and prepare seafood responsibly.
For more details, check regional sustainable seafood guides or certification websites.
